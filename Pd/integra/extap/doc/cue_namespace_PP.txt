These are the send/receive messages used in the individual cues to control DSP

Sampler:
	N=1...4	(sampler voices)
	M=1...2 (tabread outputs)
	P=1...8 (outs)

	/samN/load filename.wav

	/samN/play 1
	/samN/play 0

	/samN/chnM/outP dB msec

Harmonizer:
	N=1...3	(harmonizer voices)
	P=1...8 (outs)

	/harmN/transp cents
	
	/harmN/outP dB msec

Reverb:
	N=1...4 (reverb inputs)
	Q=1...2 (reverb outputs)
	P=1...8 (outputs)

	/rev/revtime (0-100)
	
	/rev/inN dB msec
	/rev/chnQ/outP db msec

Delay
	N=1...4 (delay voices)
	M=1...4 (delay inputs)
	P=1...8 (outputs)

	/delN/deltime msec
	/delN/inM dB msec
	/delN/outP dB msec

Filtre-ecriture
	/filt_ecr/freq hz
	/filt_ecr/dry dB msec
	/filt_ecr/wet dB msec

Filtre-soprano2
	P=1...8 (outputs)
	/filt_sop2/hp_freq hz
	/filt_sop2/lp_freq hz
	/filt_sop2/outP dB msec
